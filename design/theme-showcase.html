The audit console, distilled to its parts.

A dark-first operator aesthetic: a steady teal carries the chrome and controls, while four severity colors — red, amber, sky, slate — are reserved to mean something (the log level), never decoration. Data is set in monospace for scanning and alignment; chrome is a humanist sans. Toggle the theme top-right; every hex below updates to the active mode.
